Logistic regression is a classification algo

algorithm draws random line by taking random coefficients and check whether the points lie on posiitve region or the negative region of the line and transform the line in such a way that line passes in between the points 

if any negative point is in the positive take 1 as a coefficient to make it 3 coefficient and substract it with coefficients the line equation you can see negative and positive regions of a line on desmos of a random line 2x+3y+5>0

By default there are 1000 iterations in this algo to change coefficients

NOTE - accuracy is the metric of classification

accuracy = (Total correct predictions) / (total predictions)
